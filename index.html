Steps to Donating


Follow these steps and start donating!

1. Download the app


Go to the Google Play store and download the Donate it app.

2. Item information


Upload photographs of the item you want to donate, along with a description of the item and your contact details.

3. Notification


Get notified when one of our partnered charities has chosen your item. Arrange a collection and be proud that you are helping someone who needs it.

Creation Timeline

How the Donate it app became possible.

1. An Idea


One day we came across a problem. In the UK, more than 300,000 tonnes of reusable furniture is thrown away each year. We decided to create something that could help to solve this problem, and so Donate it was established.

2. Apps for Good Winner 2016


We entered the National Apps for Good competition and became finalists for the second time in 2016. Donate it won in the Sustainability category, thus the journey began. Our application was created by Simon Keating and we had ongoing discussions with well known charities.

3. Apps for Good Winner 2018


We re-entered into Apps for Good in 2018, however for the Legacy award. Donate it won this category, and meetings were held with Apps for Good and Virgin Red in regards to the future progression of Donate it.

4. Donate it became official


We were delighted to collaborate with Apps for Good, and with the help of Freddie Norton we made Donate it official. We are also now working with Young Enterprise to improve our platform management from a business perspective.

5. Future Plans in the Making


We are extremely ambitious about Donate it, and have many goals for the future. Our main goal, is to make this application the number 1 item donation platform in the Nation.

What's Next?

The next steps for Donate it...

Build connections


We are seeking more partners with charities, businesses and sponsors to increase the overall amount of donations facilitated through the app.

Expand our reach


Donate it is currently based in the Greater Manchester area. As we grow, building our team and partners, we will be able to support nation-wide donations.

Launch on iOS


Our application is currently only available on the Google Play store. We want to ensure that Donate it is accessable to everyone, and so we have been developing an iOS application for Apple users.

Innovate and Incorporate


In order for Donate it to grow and thrive, we must continue to innovate new and improved ideas. We have envisioned various concepts to further develop Donate it, once the platform has a variety of charities, sponsors, and nation-wide users.
